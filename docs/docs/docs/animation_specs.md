# Animation Specifications  
## Carbon Luxury Display – NZXT Elite 280

---

## General Settings
- Resolution: 480×480
- Frame rate: 30 FPS
- Looping: Seamless
- Compression: Balanced (no banding)

---

## Boot Animation
File: `boot_luxury_ignition.gif`

- Duration: 2.5 seconds
- Fade in from pure black
- Logo opacity max: 90%
- Text appears after 1.2s
- No sudden brightness changes

---

## Idle Animation
File: `m_stripes_carbon_flow.gif`

- Loop duration: 8 seconds
- Horizontal movement only
- Opacity: 30–35%
- Motion curve: Linear or ease-in-out

---

## Temperature Warning
File: `temp_warning_soft_red.gif`

- Trigger: >80°C
- Red overlay opacity: 10–15%
- Pulse duration: 3 seconds
- Fade out smoothly when temperature drops

---

## What to Avoid
- High contrast flickering
- Rapid pulses
- Neon or saturated reds

Luxury is communicated through control.
